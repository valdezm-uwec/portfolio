About

Marisa Valdez is a passionate graphic design student at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire who is pursuing a B.F.A. degree with a minor in multimedia communication.

Graphic design, at its core, is communication. It solves problems, brings people together, and most importantly, relays a specific message. When completing a project, Marisa's main goal is to translate the client's verbal information into a visual output that precisely communicates what was originally intended.

This website serves as a home base for all information regarding Marisa's creative endeavors as both a student and an individual. She sincerely hopes that you will depart feeling inspired and—dare she say—delighted.

Thank you for being a part of her journey!
